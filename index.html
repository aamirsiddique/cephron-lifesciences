    About Cephron LifeSciences

    ISO 9001:2015 Certified pharmaceutical company committed to delivering excellence in healthcare

    Fast-growing pharmaceutical and healthcare distribution company based in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh.

    Founded in July 2025, Cephron Lifesciences is committed to delivering high-quality, reliable pharmaceutical and medical products to pharmacies, hospitals, and healthcare providers across India.

    • Customer-Centric Approach: We prioritize our clients' needs with flexible and responsive service.
    • Regulatory Compliance: All products and operations adhere strictly to Indian pharmaceutical standards.
    • Reliable Distribution Network: Built to ensure fast, secure, and accurate delivery across regions.

    Under the leadership of Mr. Mohd Ahmad Hashmi, our founder and director, we operate with a clear vision: To support and strengthen the healthcare supply chain through trust, quality, and service excellence.
